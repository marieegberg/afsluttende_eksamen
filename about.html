WE ARE

Visual By Groos is a team with focus on creativity and a unique design with creative systemsolutions.
Visual By Groos innovative product solution helps you to solve the challenge of creating more space and room for your creativity. We use in our team an expression – that our focus is to create products that goes hand-in-hand with our passion to create a unique designs and craftsmanship.


A BRAND NEW IDEA

The unique design and product solution for storage of your art has not been created before – and it means that we can accommodate your needs with the new ART TROLLEY.
The innovative and brand new design therefore goes hand in hand with the new ART TROLLEY.

THE TROLLEY FOR YOUR ART

Visual By Groos new idea and brand ART TROLLEY and WALL MOUNT.
It is for art paintings to be seen as a clear solution to the problems and challenges that may occurs in connection with the storage of art paintings in your gallery or for you as an artist.
