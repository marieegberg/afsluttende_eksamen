THE FUNDAMENT FOR THE ART TROLLEY

Find out which ART TROLLEY that fit your needs. Visual By Groos have designed four different ART TROLLEYS for amateur artist and the professionals.
The ART TROLLEY is our bid for the solution of your challenge to find space for your art. See the illustration below that illustrates the models ELLIPSE, CIRCLE, SQUARE and WALL MOUNT and read more about the different types.



DIFFERNET VARIANTS

Our aim with the different variants of the ART TROLLEY has been creating precisely the ART TROLLEY which matches your needs. We want to give you a unique product with different shapes, colours and features, so you can just get the ART TROLLEY which fit into your everyday life and to your art.

ELLIPSE


The ART TROLLEY ELLIPSE with wheels is an innovative product that gives you the opportunity to keep order in your art paintings, so you always are able to find them. This ART TROLLEY gives you even more space in your gallery, and at exhibitions or in your home. ELLIPSE is the largest ART TROLLEY size 100 x 80 cm with aluminum hangers which are adjustable and divides your art, so it does not become damaged. In addition, it also has two drawers where you can keep your art supplies.

CIRCLE


ART TROLLEY CIRCLE is presented in the size 80 cm x 80 cm with wheels, so you can easily move your art paintings around from A to B. CIRCLE allows you to keep your larger art paintings particularly for professional use. At the same time, is the aesthetic and beautiful in its round expression – and is a piece of art in itself. CIRCLE consists of adjustable aluminum hangers, that can be adapted in proportion to the sizes of your art paintings as well as drawers for your art supplies.

WALL MOUNT


The WALL MOUNT is for you – which especially create smaller paintings and who don't have so much space. WALL MOUNT is also for you - who would like to present your art paintings in an aesthetic way. MATERIALS

We use the material “plywood” dark and light, which is created by thin layer of veneer, glued together with the direction of the grains. Plywood is one of the oldest of the industrially produced plate materials of wood, which is on the market today. Our choice of the material of wood is linked to the fact, that it is by far the best material both in relation to the society's economic- and in a lifecycle perspective, and that is why the choice of the material wood for us is crucial.
